Bob Parr

Robert "Bob" Parr, also known as Mr. Incredible, is a fictional superhero who appears in Disney/Pixar's 6th animated film The Incredibles and its sequel and 20th animated film Incredibles 2. He is a superhuman that has superhuman strength, durability, and stamina.

Elastigirl

Helen Parr, also known as Elastigirl and Mrs. Incredible, is a fictional superhero who appears in Pixar's animated superhero film The Incredibles and its sequel Incredibles 2.

Dash

Dashiell Robert Parr is a fictional character created by Brad Bird for the Pixar franchise The Incredibles. The character is voiced by Spencer Fox in the first film and Huckleberry Milner in the second film. Restless, relentless and curious, Dash sports a hearty sense of adventure and a boundless supply of energy.

Violet Parr

Violet Parr is a fictional character in Pixar's computer animated superhero film The Incredibles and its sequel Incredibles 2. The eldest child of Bob and Helen Parr, Violet is born with the superhuman ability to render herself invisible, as well as generate force fields
